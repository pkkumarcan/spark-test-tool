## Persona Matrix
- **Persona A (Foundational):** Seeking high-level understanding and conceptual frameworks.
- **Persona B (Intermediate):** Focuses on practical checklists, configurations, and step-by-step logic.
- **Persona C (Advanced):** Demands technical depth, deep architectures, and case studies.


For the specific implementation of Audience Segmentation on the Build With AI channel, it is highly critical to construct a robust operational framework.
