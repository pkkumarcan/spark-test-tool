## 🎨 GLOBAL STYLING CONVENTIONS
* **No Gradients:** All thumbnails and graphic slides must use flat solid colors or textured backgrounds matching the channel's hex lock. Gradients are banned.
* **Character Aesthetic:** Every avatar is explicitly faceless, designed in a colored pencil, charcoal, ink, or wash sketch style on textured paper. Lip sync or detailed facial expressions are avoided.
