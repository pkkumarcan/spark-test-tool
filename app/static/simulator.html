💡 Architectural Insight for RTX 3090 Content Creators

On the **595 branch**, if a model exceed your remaining VRAM capacity, you will experience an immediate CUDA_OUT_OF_MEMORY error.

The **610 branch** acts as a safety net: it automatically maps excess allocations to system RAM, preventing crashes but incurring a massive performance penalty (Method A).

To maintain high speeds, configure your runners (Ollama Modelfiles or llama.cpp flags) to fit at least 70% of the model layers on-card (Method B).
