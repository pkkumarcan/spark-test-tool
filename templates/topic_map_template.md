## 1. Scoring Framework

Each episode concept is evaluated across the following dimensions on a 1–5 scale:
*   **CPM Potential:** Expected ad rates in the target niche.
*   **Search Volume:** Search interest for primary keywords.
*   **Trend Momentum:** Speed at which search volume or discussion is growing.
*   **Production Ease:** Feasibility of visual asset generation (charts, avatars, B-roll).
*   **Unique Angle:** Level of counter-intuitive focus relative to competitors.
